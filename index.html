About the Book

In the age of kings and crusades, dragons ruled the skies and kingdoms fell under their fire. Rise of the Dragons is a gripping tale of knights, betrayal, and ancient magic, where one warrior must decide whether to fight for his crown or for the survival of the last dragon.

About the Author

Seraphina Blackthorn is an award-winning fantasy author known for her vivid world-building and complex characters. With a background in medieval history, she brings authenticity to her tales of dragons and knights. When not writing, she can be found studying ancient manuscripts or hiking in the Scottish Highlands.
